Featured Work

Ancestors of Tomorrow

Deep in the Amazon rainforest, the Paiter-Suruí people are fighting to protect their land, their language, and their future — on their own terms. Filmed over two years, this feature documentary follows three generations of one family as they navigate the collision between ancient tradition and an encroaching modern world.

Director: Jordan Quellman

DP: Jordan Quellman

Location: Rondônia, Brazil
